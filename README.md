## 📖 About The Venture
**AbotTanim** is an AI-integrated digital marketplace and strategic platform designed to strengthen and modernize the Philippine agricultural sector. Its primary purpose is to directly connect farmers with buyers through a mobile and web-based system, effectively minimizing the role of traditional middlemen.

This direct connection addresses two persistent issues in the sector: low farmer income and food insecurity. The platform ensures transparent transactions while providing real-time market data to support informed decision-making for both farmers and buyers.

## 📉 The Problem
The current agricultural supply chain suffers from several systemic inefficiencies:
* Currently, 27.2% of farmers struggle to cover rising production costs.
* Farmers lack real-time data and depend heavily on predatory middlemen.
* This results in a broken system where farmers earn less while buyers pay more.
* There is no direct communication channel between farmers and buyers.

## ✨ Key Features & Unique Value Proposition
AbotTanim is not merely a digital listing platform; it integrates artificial intelligence and data-driven tools to reduce farming risks and improve overall profitability:

* 🤖 **AI & Image Recognition:** The platform utilizes crop image recognition to help buyers easily identify agricultural products and provides smart recommendations for efficient sourcing.
* 📈 **Predictive Analytics:** Employs predictive AI models based on historical data and market trends to guide farmers in selecting the most suitable crops to plant and determining the optimal time to sell.
* ⚖️ **Direct & Transparent Marketplace:** Includes a real-time market price tracker and direct contracting tools to promote fair pricing and reduce price manipulation.
* 🚚 **Truck Sharing System:** Introduces a shared logistics feature that allows users to coordinate transportation resources, reducing logistics costs and minimizing post-harvest losses.
* 🗺️ **Product Heatmaps:** Provides regional heatmaps that visualize supply and demand across different areas, helping users identify market gaps and oversupply risks.
* 👥 **Community & Market Alignment:** Includes a Community Forum and a live Buyer Demand Feed, enabling farmers to exchange knowledge and align production directly with actual market needs.

## 📊 Market Opportunity
Our platform addresses a massive, scalable market within the Philippines:
* **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** Approximately 7.4 million farmers in the Philippines.
* **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** Around 3 million farmers who can realistically adopt a digital agricultural marketplace.
* **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** Targeting an initial 90,000 farmers, representing a 3% early adoption rate.

## 🎯 Target Market
AbotTanim is designed to support a sustainable ecosystem composed of two main user groups:
1.  **Farmers:** Agricultural producers seeking higher income through better planning, improved pricing, and crop diversification.
2.  **Businesses and Buyers:** Individual consumers and commercial entities looking for fresher produce, lower costs, and a transparent supply chain.

## 💡 Competitive Advantage
* **Action-Oriented vs. Advisory-Only:** Unlike indirect competitors (such as WeFarm or BayAni) that are strictly advisory or info-only, AbotTanim drives actual market transactions with integrated logistics and AI pricing.
* **Feature Exclusivity:** It is the only platform offering a powerful combination of AI Crop Recognition, demand heatmaps, and truck-sharing.

## 🚀 Go-To-Market Strategy
Our phased rollout strategy ensures steady growth and adoption:
* **Leads:** Partnering with agricultural cooperatives in Laguna and Nueva Ecija, securing LGU endorsements, and running free localized demos.
* **Prospects:** Onboarding Metro Manila restaurants and MSMEs, waiving first-transaction fees to encourage sign-ups, and utilizing targeted Facebook Groups.
* **Customers:** Launching a dedicated B2C app for household buyers and securing long-term B2B supply contracts with institutions.
